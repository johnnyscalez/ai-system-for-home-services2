**2026-05-30**: Initial research — InboxReads has high product velocity (8 features shipped Feb-May 2026). No API/webhooks/Zapier/MCP per changelog. Pricing tiers verified from /pro page: Free / Lite $8 ($90/yr) / Basic $33 ($400/yr, "Recommended") / Team $58 ($700/yr, 5 seats). Lite plan caps are tight (10 messages, 100 searches, 1 collection, 1 alert) — most active users will outgrow it within weeks. InboxReads also operates a 100+ tool catalog with reviews and comparisons; treat as curated content, not unbiased reviews. Newsletter acquisition marketplace ("Newsletters for Sale") requires due diligence on creator-claimed metrics.
