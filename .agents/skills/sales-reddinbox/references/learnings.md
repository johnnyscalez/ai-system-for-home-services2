# Reddinbox Learnings

Accumulated tips, gotchas, and discoveries from real usage. Append new findings with date.

## 2026-05-05 — Initial research

- No API, webhooks, Zapier, or MCP server exists. Entirely UI-driven.
- Quora listed as "coming soon" but not live as of research date.
- Intent scoring uses 1-100 scale but urgency language can inflate scores for non-buyers.
- Market brief limits are hard monthly caps (3 Starter, 5 Pro).
- Free tools at /free-tools work without login — useful for quick validation before paying.
- Platform positions itself as "audience intelligence" (upstream research) vs "social listening" (brand monitoring). Different use case than Brand24/Mention/Awario.
