# FieldBuilt Positioning — The Holy Trio (Time · Money · Revenue)

## The position
FieldBuilt is the employee you could never hire: one that answers every lead in a second,
never sleeps, never asks for a raise — and books the jobs into the CRM you already run.

The trio is one sentence with three consequences: it answers instantly (**revenue**),
so your office stops chasing leads (**time**), and you stop paying for ads that die
on hold (**money**). Revenue leads; the other two follow as proof.

## The centerpiece question
> What would actually change if every lead — 2 PM or 2 AM — got answered in one second,
> qualified in four questions, followed up eleven times, and booked onto the right tech's
> calendar before your competitor's phone even rang?

## The stats (cite on-page, small print)
- **78%** of homeowners hire the first company that responds (pipelineon.com)
- **12%** of contractors respond within 5 minutes; most common response time = 1 day
  (Hatch, 132,188 HVAC campaigns — caseyresponse.com)
- **391%** conversion lift when responding within 60 seconds (Velocify — leadwinner.ai)
- **21x** more likely to qualify a lead at 5 min vs 30 min (MIT — getnextphone.com)
- **62% vs 28%** close rate at 2-min vs 42-min response; on 80 leads/mo ≈ **$381K/yr** gap
  (conversionsurgery.io)

## Headlines (Ogilvy school, ranked by contractor attention)
1. **HOMEPAGE HERO (live):** "You don't have a leads problem. You have a 'nobody answered'
   problem." — starts an argument with what the owner already believes; can't be left unresolved.
2. **Proof punch (live as hero stat blocks):** 62% vs 28% / 78% first responder / $381K gap.
3. **After-hours campaign ad:** "At 2 a.m., the loudest sound in your office is a customer
   hanging up."
4. **Ads/cold email:** "What time does your office close? Your leads don't know. Neither do
   your competitors."
5. **Anti-SaaS differentiator:** "This is not software. Software waits for you to log in.
   This clocks in when you clock out."
6. **Outcome:** "Every morning, jobs on the board that nobody in your company touched."
7. **Stat pair:** "Only 12% of contractors answer a lead within five minutes. They take home
   78% of the jobs."

## Discipline
Never "revolutionary," "game-changing," or "AI-powered" as a benefit. Every claim is a
number, a time of day, or something the reader can check. Confidence lives in specificity.
Claim standard: "under 60 seconds" for product claims; "one second" only inside the
hypothetical question.
